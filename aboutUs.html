The journey of Restaurant ORA begins in the 50s when Mr. Sabit Hoti started working at Hotel Llapi. He did not remain a simple worker because his ambitions of a private entrepreneur did not leave him alone. In 1967, he opened the first private coffee-tea house in Podujevë, and from that day the "Sabah" tea house, or as it is known by the people, "Sabit Hoti Coffee" became a point of reference in the entire region of Llapi and beyond.

Today, the long-standing tradition continues, and the spirit of Albanian hospitality is present in the large family with a tradition of hospitality. Today, in memory of Mr. Sabit Hoti, the family built the most luxurious restaurant in the Llap region and one of the best restaurants in Kosovo.

Restaurant ORA, has the facility with all the modern infrastructure with an area of ​​3,600 m2, with a wonderful environment, exceptional lighting, air conditioning and ventilation with the highest standards, with a perfect acoustic system and with a total capacity of up to 1,500 people.
